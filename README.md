# Trading Zone

**Trading Zone** is a repository dedicated to collecting and organizing financial data, market tickers, and resources for technical analysis.

It includes datasets, scripts, and tools designed to support traders, analysts, and market enthusiasts in their research, backtesting, and trading strategies.

## Contents

- 📊 Financial datasets and time series  
- 📈 Market tickers and instrument references  
- 🧮 Scripts for analysis, data processing, and indicators  
- 🛠️ Utilities and tools for trading research and strategy development  

## Goals

The goal of this repository is to provide a structured and reusable knowledge base for:
- Technical analysis studies  
- Strategy development and testing  
- Market data exploration  
- Trading system research  

## Intended Audience

- Traders (discretionary or systematic)  
- Quantitative analysts  
- Developers working on trading tools  
- Finance and market enthusiasts  

## Disclaimer

This repository is intended for **educational and research purposes only**.  
Nothing contained here should be considered financial advice.
